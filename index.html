About Me

Hello! My is Gaurav Chatterjee, and I am from Randolph, MA, which is a couple of towns south of Boston.

I am currently a junior at Carnegie Mellon University. I entered CMU as a Civil & Environmental Engineering major, but after being surrounded by people focused on computers and technology, I too developed an interest in programming and computer engineering. Thus, I added an additional major in Computer Science.

Outside of class, I am the one of the co-chairs of my school's team in ASCE Student Conferences. We compete in competitions including Steel Bridge, Concrete Bat, Concrete Bowling, and Environmental.
